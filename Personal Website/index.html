臺灣鳳梨產業概況與交易價量分析 ｜ Taiwan Pineapple Industry Overview and Transaction Analysis

Spring 2026
  • 本報告針對臺灣最具代表性的經濟果樹——鳳梨，進行了深度的產業運銷與市場趨勢分析。透過整合農業部與批發市場的歷史交易數據，探討鳳梨在臺灣各區域的生產集中度、季節性供需失衡對價格波動的影響以及進出口貿易結構的重大轉變。報告結合視覺化圖表與盒鬚圖分析，釐清了核心批發市場的交易特徵與定價機制，並梳理出國內農產品行口體系與多元通路的運銷網絡。
  • This report provides an in-depth analysis of the production, distribution, and market trends of Taiwan's most representative economic fruit, Pineapple. By integrating historical transaction data from the Ministry of Agriculture and wholesale markets, the report examines regional production concentration, the impact of seasonal supply-demand imbalances on price volatility, and significant structural shifts in export and import trade. Utilizing data visualization and box plot analysis, the study clarifies the transaction characteristics and pricing mechanisms of core wholesale markets, and maps out the domestic agricultural product distribution system and diversified marketing network.

標普500行業子指數的指數增強策略 ｜ Enhanced Index Strategy Based on S&P 500 Sub-Index

Winter 2026
  • 本研究旨在探討宏觀經濟指標與標普 500 中 11 個行業子指數之間的連動關係，並以此建構指數增強策略。研究核心在於量化各產業對消費者物價指數 (CPI)、聯邦基金利率 (FFR) 及美元指數 (DXY) 變動的敏感度，利用歷史相關係數與標準化因子建立評分機制，篩選出在不同宏觀環境下具備超額報酬潛力的產業組合，實現動態的行業輪動。
  • This project investigates the dynamic relationships between macroeconomic indicators and the eleven S&P 500 industry sub-indices to develop an enhanced index strategy. The methodology focuses on quantifying sector sensitivity to shifts in the Consumer Price Index (CPI), Federal Funds Rate (FFR), and the US Dollar Index (DXY). By integrating historical correlation coefficients with standardized macro factor changes (Z-scores), the study establishes a scoring framework to identify sector combinations with the potential for outperformance across different economic regimes, in order to implement systematic sector rotation.

美國女性就業與勞動力參與率展望：與 OECD 國家之對比分析 ｜ The Outlook for Women’s Employment and Labor Force Participation: A Comparative Analysis between USA and OECD Countries

Fall 2024
  • 本報告旨在分析過去 30 年間，美國女性勞動力參與率停滯不前的原因，並探討為何其表現逐漸落後於多數 OECD 國家。透過量化數據分析，報告深入探討了薪資結構變化、教育程度影響、所得稅制度以及家庭支持政策（如育兒成本及有薪產假）對女性投入職場意願的關鍵影響。
  • This report explores the factors behind the stagnation of women’s labor force participation in the United States over the last three decades, contrasting it with the rising trends in other OECD nations. Through quantitative analysis, the report investigates how evolving earnings structures, educational attainment, taxation systems, and the absence of robust family support policies—including high childcare costs and lack of paid parental leave—serve as significant barriers to women’s economic engagement.

臺灣水泥產業市值與獲利指標之關聯統計分析 ｜ Statistical Analysis of the Correlation between Market Value and Profitability Indicators in Taiwan's Cement Industry

Fall 2023
  • 本報告旨在探討臺灣水泥產業中，各項獲利指標（如 ROE、EPS、毛利率等）與企業市值之間的關聯性。報告採用 Python 進行統計建模，將產業分為龍頭公司與中小型公司兩大類群進行對比分析。透過相關係數熱力圖、多元線性迴歸及殘差分析，我們不僅建立了預測模型，更深入探討了如綠色轉型投資等質性因素如何影響獲利指標與市值之間的數值表現。
  • This report investigates the correlation between profitability indicators (such as ROE, EPS, and margins) and corporate market capitalization within Taiwan's cement industry. Utilizing Python for statistical modeling, the research segments the industry into "Market Leaders" and "SMEs" for comparative analysis. Through correlation heatmaps, multiple linear regression, and residual diagnostics, we established predictive models and explored how qualitative factors, such as "green transition investments," influence the quantitative relationship between earnings and market valuation.

臺灣汽車產業財務報表分析 ｜ Financial Statement Analysis of Taiwan's Automotive Industry

Spring 2023
  • 本報告針對臺灣汽車市場兩大龍頭——和泰汽車與裕隆汽車，進行了 2018 至 2022 年間的全面性財務評估。透過垂直分析、水平分析、財務比率分析及杜邦分析法，深入探討兩家企業在經營績效、獲利品質與償債能力上的差異。報告也特別剖析了外部環境（如新冠疫情、車用晶片短缺）及特殊事件（如和泰防疫險風波）對財務表現的實質衝擊。

Utilizing vertical, horizontal, financial ratio, and DuPont analysis, we conducted an in-depth study of their operational performance, profitability, and solvency. The analysis specifically highlights the financial impact of external macro-factors, such as the COVID-19 pandemic and semiconductor shortages, alongside corporate-specific events like Hotai's pandemic insurance losses.
